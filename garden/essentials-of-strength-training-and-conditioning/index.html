  • Structures of muscle and bone#

    • Muscle belly → fascicle → fiber → myofibril → myofilaments（actin & myosin）

    • 結締組織：

      • Epimysium（外層）

      • Perimysium（包住fascicle） 同時負責拉tendon

      • Endomysium（包住fiber）

    • Sarcomere 是肌肉的收縮單位，內含 actin（細）與 myosin（粗）

    • skeleton:

      • 骨骼肌只能牽引（pull）不能推（push）

      • 分成 axial（頭骨、脊椎、胸骨）與 appendicular（四肢） clavicle is appendicular

      • 關節分類：

        • Fibrous（無活動度，如頭骨縫）

        • Cartilaginous（有限活動，如椎間盤）

        • Synovial（高度活動，如膝、肩）→ 運動訓練重點關節

  • Sliding-filament theory of muscular contraction#

    • 收縮步驟：

      • Ca²⁺ 釋放→ troponin 結合→ tropomyosin 移位

      • Myosin ATPase 活化：cross-bridge 與 actin 結合

      • Power stroke（動力滑動）：actin 被拉向中間（Z-line 接近）

      • 需新 ATP 解開 cross-bridge

      • Ca²⁺ + ATP 缺一不可！

    • The number of crossbridges that are formed between actin and myosin at any instant in time dictates the force production of a muscle.

    • ▶️ Muscle Contraction – 收縮循環#

Phase關鍵事件
Resting無 Ca²⁺，肌絲未結合
Excitation-ContractionCa²⁺ 結合 troponin → myosin 結合 actin
ContractionATP 分解，myosin 拉動 actin
Recharge新 ATP 解開並重設
RelaxationCa²⁺ 回收至 SR，actin-myosin 分離

  • Neuromuscular System（神經肌肉系統）#

    • Motor Unit = 1 個運動神經元 + 所有支配的 muscle fibers

    • **All-or-none 原則：**所有 fiber 同時收縮或不收縮

      • stronger action potential ≠ stonger contraction

    • 精細動作：每個 motor neuron 控制 1–2 fibers（如眼肌）

    • 粗大動作：每個控制數百 fibers（如股四頭肌）

  • Levers of the Musculoskeletal System#

    • 定義

      • Torque（轉矩） = Force × Moment Arm

      • Moment arm（力臂） = 力量作用線到旋轉中心的垂直距離

      • Mechanical Advantage（機械優勢） = 力臂比值 $(M_{AF}/M_{RF})$

      • > 1：肌力小於阻力即可平衡（第二類槓桿）

      • < 1：肌力需大於阻力（人體大多為第三類槓桿）

    • 三類槓桿系統

    • | 型 | 結構順序（力點/支點/阻力） | 範例 | 優劣 | | 第1類 | 力 – 支點 – 阻力 | 頭部仰俯 triceps | 看 MA | | 第2類 | 支點 – 阻力 – 力 | 小腿踮腳 | 力省 | | 第3類 | 支點 – 力 – 阻力 | 手肘彎舉 | 力不省但速度快（人體大多是此型） |

  • Human Strength and Power#

    • Force = Mass ∙ Acceleration

    • Work = Force ∙ Displacement（Joule, J）

    • Power = Work / Time (Watt, W) = 1J/sec

    • Work = Torque ∙ Angular displacement

  • Strength Versus Power#

    • Strength（肌力）：在任何速度下產生**最大力量（force）**的能力

    • Power（爆發力）：單位時間產生的力量×速度 → Power = Force × Velocity (distance/time)

    • The sport of weightlifting (Olympic lifting) has a much higher power component than the sport of powerlifting, due to the higher movement velocities with heavy weights of the weightlifting movements

  • 決定力量與爆發力的因素#

    • Neural Control

      • 控制招募哪些 motor units、數量與發放頻率（rate coding）

      • 初期訓練（前幾週）之進步來自神經適應而非肌肥大

    • 肌肉**橫斷面積 **

      • 肌肉產力 ∝ 橫斷面積，與體積無直接相關

+ 肌肉角度跟起止點位置之間的角度 **Angle of Pennation（羽狀角）**


  + Pennation angle = 肌纖維與肌腱軸線的夾角

  + 平行肌（fusiform）：pennation angle ≈ 0°

羽狀肌（pennate muscle）：肌纖維以角度插入腱膜（如 vastus lateralis）

  + **Pennation angle ↑ → 可容納更多纖維** → ↑ 力，但 ↓ 速度

  + 重量訓練可**改變 pennation angle** → 訓練後 **pennation angle ↑**，肌力輸出 ↑

是一種結構性適應（與肌肥大並行）

+ Muscle Length : 


  + **Resting length**：最理想的 actin-myosin 重疊 → 力量最強

  + 過度拉長或過度縮短 → cross-bridge 數減少 → 力 ↓

+ Joint Angle


  + Torque（轉矩）隨 joint angle 改變而異

  + 存在「最佳角度」可產生最大力

+ 肌肉收縮速度


  + 縮得越快 → **力量產出 ↓**

  + **向心收縮**：速度 ↑ → 力量 ↓

  + **離心收縮**：速度 ↓  → 力量 ↑（但 >90°/s後會下降）

  + the greatest muscle force can be obtained during eccentric muscle action.

  • Sources of Resistance to Muscle Contraction#

  • 常見阻力來源（5種）#

類型解釋特性與訓練應用
Gravity地心引力產生的垂直向下力量（Fg = m × g）自由重量訓練基本來源；力矩取決於重量與水平方向距關節距離
Inertia移動質量時，需額外力量改變其加速度（F = ma）例如啞鈴啟動時需額外出力；適用爆發訓練、需控制啟動和停止
Friction運動部件間的摩擦阻力常見於滑輪或器材移動部件；阻力與速度無關
Fluid Resistance液體或氣體流體阻力（例：游泳、划船、氣壓式機械）抵抗力 ∝ 移動速度（FR = k × v）；無離心階段，常為 concentric only
Elasticity彈性材料（如彈力帶）回復力阻力隨拉伸↑；高點張力高，訓練末端難度加大

Ch.3 Bioenergetics#

  • terminology

    • Bioenergetics：轉換食物能量 → 生理可用能量（主要是 ATP）

    • Catabolism：分解反應，釋放能量（ex: 肌肉收縮用的 ATP 水解）

    • Anabolism：合成反應，消耗能量（ex: 合成蛋白）

    • Exergonic：釋能（→ 提供能量給其他反應）

    • Endergonic：需能反應（ex: 合成、肌肉收縮）

    • Metabolism = 所有分解 + 合成反應總和

    • ATP（Adenosine Triphosphate）

      • 組成：Adenosine（adenine + ribose）+ 三個 phosphate

      • ATP 水解（ATPase）：ATP + H₂O → ADP + Pi + H⁺ + energy

      • 功用：中介 exergonic → endergonic 過程，缺 ATP → 肌肉活動停止

  • Biological Energy Systems#

系統特性主用情境
Phosphagen system最快產生 ATP；容量小極高強度、短時間（ex: 100m、舉重）
Glycolysis快速產能、中等容量中高強度（ex: 400m、重量訓練）
Oxidative system產能慢、但容量最大長時間低強度（ex: 慢跑、鐵人三項）

  • Phosphagen system#

    • $\mathrm{ADP} + \mathrm{Creatine Phosphate (CP)}\xleftrightarrow{\text{Creatine Kinase}}$\mathrm{ATP} + \mathrm{Creatine Phosphate (CP)}

    • provides ATP primarily for short-term, high-intensity activities

    • highly active at the start of all exercise regardless of intensity

    • Another important single-enzyme reaction that can rapidly replenish ATP is the adenylate kinase (also called myokinase) reaction $$2\mathrm{ADP} \xleftrightarrow{\text{adenylate kinase}} \mathrm{ATP} + \mathrm{AMP}$$

    • AMP is a powerful stimulant of glycolysis

    • 這是 near-equilibrium reactions : 被物質濃度控制平衡的方向

  • Glycolysis#

    • 分解 glucose/glycogen → pyruvate

    • pyruvate 繼續proceed：兩個方向

      • → Lactate（乳酸） = Fast Glycolysis（快速但限時）→ 無氧代謝 恢復 NAD+ 量但產生 H+ ↓ pH

      • → Mitochondria = Slow Glycolysis（慢但可長時）→ 有氧代謝

    • 產生 ATP 比 CP 慢，但原料多 → 可支持較長運動

    • Lactate#

      • Lactate , not lactic acid is the end product. (lactate dehydrogenase)

+ #### ✅ Lactate Threshold & Onset of Blood Lactate Accumulation（LT & OBLA）


  + **Lactate Threshold (LT)**：	運動時乳酸濃度首次明顯↑的點	表示開始大量依賴無氧代謝

  + **Onset of Blood Lactate Accumulation (OBLA)**：	血中乳酸達到 4 mmol/L 時	表示乳酸產生 > 清除速率，代謝壓力↑

  + LT/OBLA 發生的強度


    + LT：未訓練者：約在 50–60% VO₂max

有氧訓練者：約在 70–80% VO₂max

    + OBLA：通常在 LT 之後，是第二個乳酸迅速累積點

  + 可訓練向右推延 LT/OBLA 曲線 → 可在更高強度下仍維持有氧代謝


    + 機制：↓ catecholamine（激素壓力）反應

↑ 粒線體密度 & 有氧酵素活性 ↑ 清除乳酸能力（如 lactate shuttle, Cori cycle）

  • ▶️ Glycogen#

    • 🏃‍♂️ 消耗特性：#

    • muscle glycogen：中高強度時主要來源

    • liver glycogen：低強度、長時間運動時主力

    • 強度 ↑ → glycogen 分解速率 ↑（50%, 75%, 100% VO2max 對應速率為 0.7, 1.4, 3.4 mmol/kg/min）

  • 🔍 兩大核心：#

+ **Reliability（信賴度）** = 測驗結果是否一再一致

  • 效度類型：#

    • 類型定義範例
      Construct Validity測驗是否真正反映某抽象概念（理論核心）垂直跳測爆發力 power
      Face Validity表面看來是否合理（不等同於科學驗證）教練覺得 shuttle run 很像比賽情境
      Content Validity測驗是否涵蓋所有相關面向體能測驗是否包含力量、有氧、敏捷等
      Criterion-Referenced Validity測驗結果與已驗證標準的相關性一個新測驗與 VO₂max 之相關係數高

    • Criterion-referenced validity

      • | 類型 | 定義 | 關鍵差異 | 範例 | | —- | —- | —- | | Concurrent Validity | 測驗結果是否與目前可接受的金標準測驗一致 | ✅ 同時比對 | 垂直跳 vs. 高科技力板同步測爆發力 | | Predictive Validity | 測驗結果是否能預測未來表現 | ✅ 未來導向 | 40-yard dash 測成績是否能預測 WR 表現 | | Discriminant Validity | 測驗能否區分不同能力或族群 | ✅ 能區分才有意義 | 一項肌力測驗不應與柔軟度高度相關 |

      • Concurrent validity is the extent to which test scores are associated with those of other accepted tests that measure the same ability

        • Convergent validity is evidenced by high positive correlation between results of the test being assessed and those of the recognized measure of the construct(the “gold standard”).

        • Convergent Validity 測驗與理論上相似的其他測驗結果一致性 通常透過**相關係數（r）**檢驗，r > 0.7 通常為佳 看是否“聚合”到同構念

      • Predictive validity corresponds with future behavior or performance.

      • Discriminant validity distinguish between two different constructs and is evidenced by a low correlation between the results of the test and those of tests of a different construct

      • Concurrent = 現在 vs. 標準 → 「我這個測驗能否跟公認準的對上？」

      • Predictive = 現在 vs. 未來 → 「我這測驗能否預測誰能成功？」

      • Discriminant = 不該相關的果然無關 → 「我這測驗能否只測我想測的東西？」

    • 1RM Bench Press#

type:: test category:: strength tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Olympic barbell, flat bench, weight plates, collars, spotter arms or racks

  + 👥 Personnel: 1–2 trained spotters, 1 test administrator

  + 🔁 Procedure:

  1. General warm-up + specific warm-up（5–10 reps with light load）

  2. Rest 1 min → 3–5 reps @ ~60–70% est. 1RM

  3. Rest 2 min → 2–3 reps @ ~80% 1RM

  4. Rest 2–4 min → attempt 1RM

  5. Increase weight 5–10% per attempt until failure

  6. Full ROM: bar touches chest（no bounce）, full elbow lockout

    • 📌 Notes:

      • Male collegiate average: ~1.05×BW（good）；Elite >1.5×BW

      • Combine settings often use submax RM（e.g. 225lb reps） instead

    • 13.2 1RM Bench Pull#

type:: test alias:: category:: strength tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Flat bench with support for horizontal pull, barbell, lifting straps optional

  + 👥 Personnel: 1 administrator to confirm rep standard

  + 🔁 Procedure:

  1. Lie prone on bench, barbell racked under chest level

  2. Pull bar upward to touch underside of bench without torso motion

  3. Lower under control, full extension

  4. Use same warm-up & 1RM progression as bench press

    • ⚠️ Caveats:

      • Torso must stay in contact with bench at all times 頭也是 Overextension = invalid rep Should not allow excessive elbow flare

    • 📌 Notes:

      • Often paired with 1RM Bench Press to assess push-pull ratio Ideal Push:Pull ratio ≈ 1:1 in balanced athletes

    • 13.3 1RM Back Squat#

type:: test category:: strength tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Olympic barbell, squat rack or power cage, safety pins

  + 👥 Personnel: 2 trained spotters, 1 紀錄

  + 🔁 Procedure:

  1. Warm-up progression same as Bench Press

  2. Bar rests on upper traps（not neck）

  3. Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out

  4. Descent: thighs parallel to floor = minimum depth

  5. Ascend to full extension without bar shifting

    • ⚠️ Caveats:

      • Monitor for valgus collapse, trunk lean, and heel lift Full depth standard = thigh parallel Spotters must coordinate hand placement & timing

    • 📌 Notes:

      • Male collegiate average: 1.5–2.0×BW（elite >2.5×BW） Excellent for force development assessment Pair with VJ to evaluate strength–power profile

  + 👥 Personnel: 1 tester/recorder

    • 13.10 300-Yard Shuttle#

type:: test alias:: Anaerobic Capacity Shuttle category:: anaerobic capacity tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Stopwatch, 25-yard marked lanes, cones

  + 👥 Personnel: 1 timer, 1 administrator

  + 🔁 Procedure:

  1. Place 2 cones 25 yards apart

  2. Sprint 25 yards out and back = 1 rep

  3. Complete 6 total reps (6 × 50y = 300 yards)

  4. Time entire effort with stopwatch

  5. Perform 2 trials separated by full recovery（~5 mins）

  6. Average of both trials is score

    • ⚠️ Caveats: Turns must be sharp, touching or crossing line Test must be conducted on flat, non-slip surface Fatigue may cause pacing—encourage maximal effort

type:: test alias:: T-Agility Drill category:: agility tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: 4 cones (5-10-5 yd), stopwatch, tape measure

👥 Personnel: 1 timer, 1 administrator
🔁 Procedure:

  1. Set cones in “T” formation：10 yd top, 5 yd stem

  2. Sprint forward 10 yd, shuffle left 5 yd, shuffle right 10 yd, shuffle left 5 yd, backpedal 10 yd

  3. Must touch base of each cone with hand

  4. Time recorded from start to full return

    • ⚠️ Caveats: Hand must touch cone base or trial invalid Footwork form affects time significantly Lateral shuffles = no crossover steps

    • 13.19 Hexagon Test#

type:: test alias:: Hex Jump Test category:: agility + coordination tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Athletic tape to mark hexagon (24 in sides), stopwatch

👥 Personnel: 1 timer, 1 floor judge
🔁 Procedure:

  1. Form hexagon with 6 equal 24-in sides, 120° angles

  2. Jump over each side and back to center in clockwise direction

  3. 3 full revolutions (~18 jumps), time recorded

  4. Repeat in opposite direction (optional)

    • best time of three trials is recorded

    • ⚠️ Caveats: Only 2-foot jumps allowed Misstep = restart or penalty Requires spatial awareness and coordination

    • 📌 Notes: Tests multidirectional reactivity and lower limb stability Norm: Male ~12–13 sec; Female ~13–15 sec

  + 🛠️ Equipment: 3 cones spaced 5 yards apart, stopwatch or timing gates

👥 Personnel: 1 timer, 1 administrator
🔁 Procedure:

  1. Start straddling center cone line (three-point stance)

  2. Sprint 5 yd right, then 10 yd left, then return 5 yd to center

  3. Must touch base of each outer cone with hand

  4. Best of 2 trials recorded

    • ⚠️ Caveats: Must touch line accurately for valid rep Change-of-direction footwork affects time greatly Lateral lean control is key

type:: test alias:: Single-Direction Change of Direction category:: agility tags::

👥 Personnel: 1 timer, 1 administrator
🔁 Procedure:

  1. Set cones at start, 5m, and 15m marks

  2. Athlete sprints forward from 15m mark

  3. Time is recorded over final 10m segment (from 10m → 15m and back to 10m)

  4. At 15m cone, athlete cuts 180°, sprints back 5m

  5. Test best of two trials per leg

    • ⚠️ Caveats: Start is moving; don’t time first 10m (acceleration phase) Use both left and right leg cuts for asymmetry screening Clear cone contact and change of direction technique critical

    • 13.23 Balance Error Scoring System (BESS)#

type:: test alias:: Static Postural Stability Test category:: balance and stability tags:: [:span] ls-type:: annotation hl-page:: 300 hl-color:: yellow

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Foam pad, stopwatch, clipboard

👥 Personnel: 1 administrator to count errors

  + 🔁 Procedure:

    + 1. 3 stances × 2 surfaces (firm + foam)：

a) Double-leg stance
b) Single-leg stance (non-dominant foot)
c) Tandem (heel-to-toe) stance (dominant foot in front of the nondominant foot)

    + 2. Each held for 20 sec, eyes closed, hands on hips

    + 3. Count errors (max 10 per condition):

opening eyes; lifting hands from hips; touchdown of nonstance foot; step, hop, or other lifting forefeet or heel moving hip into more than 30° of hip flexion or abduction out of position for more than 5 seconds

    + 4. Total errors across all 6 conditions = score

  + ⚠️ Caveats:

Very sensitive to fatigue, concussion, inner ear issues Inconsistent flooring or ambient noise can affect results

  + 📌 Notes:

Widely used in concussion screening Lower total errors = better balance Norms: Healthy athletes = ~10–15 total errors

+ #### 13.24 Star Excursion Balance Test (SEBT)

type:: test alias:: Dynamic Balance Reach Test category:: balance and stability tags::

  + 🛠️ Equipment: Tape or grid to form 8-star directions (120° separation)

👥 Personnel: 1 administrator to mark distances

    + 🔁 Procedure:

  1. Athlete stands on one leg in center

  2. Reaches opposite foot in 3 main directions (anterior, posteromedial, posterolateral)

  3. Reach as far as possible while maintaining single-leg stance rest at least 15 secs

  4. Distance recorded per direction; 3 trials each (take average)

  5. Normalize score to leg length（reach ÷ limb length × 100）

    • ⚠️ Caveats: Loss of balance, foot tap, or shift = invalid trial Leg length must be accurately measured (ASIS → medial malleolus) Allow practice trials for familiarization

    • 📌 Notes: Effective for injury risk screening（esp. ankle/knee） Composite score <94% = ↑ injury risk Dynamic alternative to BESS

  • 🔥 Warm-Up#

    • 好處：分成 Temperature-related effects - ↑ muscle & core temperature, ↑ neural function, disruption of transient connective tissue bonds Non–temperature-related effects - ↑ blood flow to muscles, ↑ baseline O2 consumption, postactivation potentiation

    • ⏱目的：↑肌收縮速度、力量、傳導速率、O₂供應 (Bohr effect)，↓黏滯阻力、傷害風險 心理準備

    • The structure of the warm-up influences potential improvements; as such, the warmup needs to be specific to the activity to be performed ls-type:: annotation hl-page:: 334 hl-color:: yellow

    • 包含2部分：全程 10-20mins. 活動前 15min內 完成，太早沒用。

      • General warm-up：5–10 min 有氧(慢跑 跳繩): ↑HR、血流、肌溫、呼吸率 -> general stretching

      • Specific warm-up：動態活動，模擬比賽動作（ex: 跳躍、動態伸展）

    • 功能：動作準備、神經啟動、心肺刺激、心理集中

  • Targeted and Structured Warm-Ups#

建議結構：RAMP protocol

  + **R**aise (general warmup)： ↑體溫、HR、呼吸、血流、關節活動
  + **A**ctivate & **M**obilize (stretching)：啟動關鍵肌群（agonist, stabilizer），動態活動改善ROM

➡dynamic stretching and mobility exercises > static stretching

  + **P**otentiate (specific warm-up)：比賽前神經激發（↑神經驅動），強度遞增模擬競賽要求

➡high-intensity dynamic exercises 平常 training 時這段就可以放 speed training and agility training

+ ⚡與傳統warm-up相比，RAMP強調動作導向與個別化設計，↑效能

  • Plyometric Mechanics and Physiology#

    • 🔩 Mechanical Model#

      • 肌肉肌腱單位（MTU）內的 series elastic component (SEC) 儲存彈性能量，主要為 肌腱（tendon），而非肌肉本身

      • 當 MTU 被快速拉長（eccentric）→ SEC 儲能

      • 若迅速進入 concentric phase → 彈性能量釋放 → 產生額外張力（↑ power）（即 SSC） 延遲太久能量耗散為熱能 → 效果↓

    • ⚡ Neurophysiological Model#

      • potentiation by use of 牽張反射（stretch reflex）

      • **肌梭（muscle spindle）**感應被拉長 → 啟動 牽張反射（stretch reflex） 🧠 整合：

    • 結合 SEC 彈性張力 + CNS stretch reflex → ↑ concentric power

    • 🔁 Stretch-Shortening Cycle (SSC)#

      • 三個相連階段：

        Phase內容關鍵要素
        1️⃣ Eccentric肌肉被拉長、儲存能量preload、肌梭活化、Ia fiber
        2️⃣ Amortization切換期，需極短暫神經傳導時間（↓好）
        3️⃣ Concentric反向收縮，利用儲存能量彈力釋放 + reflex, alpha motor neuron

      • ✅ 縮短 amortization phase = 提升 SSC 效率（通常 < 0.2 秒）

      • The stretch–shortening cycle combines mechanical and neurophysiological mechanisms and is the basis of plyometric exercise. A rapid eccentric muscle action stimulates the stretch reflex and storage of elastic energy, which increase the force produced during the subsequent concentric action. ls-type:: annotation hl-page:: 490 hl-color:: yellow

+ ### 1️⃣ Long, Slow Distance (LSD)


  + Intensity：~70% VO₂max or 80% HRmax（RPE ~ 5）(Zone 2)

  + Duration：**長於比賽距離或時間**

  +

Goal：

    + ↑ aerobic base

↑ mitochondrial density, capillarization, fat utilization ↑ CV function, thermoregulation ↑ lactate threshold intensity

  + 缺點：speed 無法顯著提升, 沒有刺激 neurological recruitment

+ ### 2️⃣ Pace/Tempo Training（Threshold Training）


  + Intensity：At or slightly ↑ than lactate threshold, 「comfortably hard」(RPE 6,7) (Zone 3)

  + 形式：steady or intermittent


    + Steady（20–30 min）

    + Intermittent (tempo/cruise interval)（e.g., 2×10min w/ 2 min recovery）

  +

Goal：

    + ↑ LT（跑更快而不累）

    + ↑ running economy, mental toughness

  + 關鍵：配速感 training、忍受 steady discomfort, sustain

If the workout seems relatively easy, it is better to increase the distance than to increase the intensity. ls-type:: annotation hl-page:: 584 hl-color:: yellow

+ ### 3️⃣ Interval Training

  + Intensity：close to VO₂max（~90–100%）

  + Work:Rest = 1:1 to 1:3（視目標）

  + Duration（每組）：30s–5min (usually 3-5m)

  + Goal：

    + ↑ VO₂max

    + ↑ anaerobic tolerance

  + 注意：心肺負擔大，恢復須足夠💢

+ ### 4️⃣ High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)


  + Intensity：close to VO₂max（~90–100% HRR）加起來要 several mins 以上

  + Interval：30s–4min；rest = equal or longer

  +

Goal：

    + ↑ VO₂max

    + ↑ sprint-end power output

    + ↑ anaerobic capacity

    + ↑ running speed, economy

  + 常用於 time-efficient training & advanced athletes

+ ### 5️⃣ Fartlek Training（Speed Play）


  + Intensity：**varied**（包括 LSD 70% & tempo 85-90%）

  + Duration：20–60 min

  + 結合自由變速（如上坡、下坡、節奏變化）

  + Goal：↑ VO₂max、LT、running economy

      • single leg squat, plyometric landings

  • 🎯 High Yield / Take-Home Points#

    • Rehab = team work，S&C ≠ rehab leader，但是關鍵實踐者

    • Rehab phase 明確區分 → phase-specific training 📈

    • Healing ≠ linear progression，須隨時調整

    • 溝通流暢 = 最重要的 injury recovery 預測因子之一
